The 80s was a decade of cultural transformation and the expression of individuality. People were no longer afraid to be who they were, and that mentality was reflected in their fashion choices. They like extravagant styles, bold colors, and accessories were a must. Neon clothes and jumpsuits were a thing, too. On the other hand, American designers such as Perry Ellis and Ralph Lauren were turning to a more classic and casual style with the “preppy look”. Which was influenced by traditional Ivy League and Seven Sisters Style, as well as the dress of early  20th century British aristocrats.
